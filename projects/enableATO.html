enableATO is a project within the German Center for Future Mobility (DZM).

At RailCampus OWL in Minden, a consortium of universities, Fraunhofer institutes and companies is advancing technologies for automated rail-based mobility and researching essential underlying technologies. The focus is on automated driving, including sensor-based perception, approval processes, intelligent maintenance and technology demonstrations. Additionally, user acceptance is explored and scientific exchange is fostered. Existing projects like the driverless MONOCAB transport system and the dual-mode vehicle are tested and demonstrated in Minden and Extertal as part of enableATO.
